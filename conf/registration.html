Camera Ready Manuscript Preparation
& Submission Guide for Authors

Step 1. Prepare Camera Ready Manuscript
  • The manuscript should be STRICTLY prepared using LaTeX or MS-Word templates given below (download the template for your use, please do not request for edit permissions)
        
  • All comments provided by reviewers must be addressed fully and a response sheet should be prepared.
  • The manuscript should not have a similarity level of more than 20% including publications/submissions of self.
  • All authors’ affiliation information should be complete and correct. Please note, any changes (addition, deletion or rearrangement) in authorship cannot be made after the acceptance of the paper. Only in exceptional cases, it can be permitted by proceedings editors.

Step 3. Camera-Ready Submission
  • Authors need to submit following files during Camera-Ready Submission:
      Camera-Ready Manuscript File (pdf) [XXX_Manuscript.pdf]
      Reviewer Response Summary (pdf) [XXX_ ResponseSummary.pdf]
      Camera-Ready Manuscript Source Files (LaTeX or MS-Word) and original images (please ensure that all the figures are of 300 DPI resolutions as this will facilitate good output and files are named as fig1.jpg, fig2.png, fig3.jpeg) [XXX_SourceFiles.zip]
      [Optional] If applicable, student-Id card to avail student registration benefits (only student can present the paper) [XXX_SID.pdf]
      Payment receipt (acknowledgement generated from bank) [XXX_PaymentReceipt.pdf]

    NOTE: Please rename your files as per mentioned convention where XXX is your submission id in three digits. For example, if your submission id is 9, please rename your files as follows:

    009_Manuscript.pdf,     009_ResponseSummary.pdf,     009_SourceFiles.zip,   
    009_SID.pdf,                009_PaymentReceipt.pdf
