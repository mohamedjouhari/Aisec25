Welcome to AISEC 2025

Attracting a global audience, the International Conference on Artificial Intelligence and Cybersecurity (AISEC 2025) is a premier event in the fields of artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, and sustainable technologies. It aims to showcase cutting-edge research and innovative solutions in AI-driven cybersecurity, addressing the challenges and opportunities in securing digital systems. The AISEC 2025 will be held at the National School of Applied Sciences (ENSA), Marrakech, Morocco, on May 12 - 14, 2025, in collaboration with leading research institutions and industry partners. Join us as we explore the future of intelligent and secure systems in one of the most vibrant cities in the world.

  • Location ENSA, Marrakech, Morocco
  • Date & Time 10am - 7pm, 12th-14th, May
  • Speakers 70 Professionals
  • Seats 200 People

Call For Papers

  • The contributions describing original, state-of-the art unpublished research, and not currently under review by another conference or journal and addressing the latest and current research are invited in AISEC 2025.
  • Best student paper award will be conferred at the conference.
  • For PhD best dissertation award the doctoral candidate is required to submit a one-page nomination letter signed by his/her Supervisor/Department or Institute Head.
  • Topics of interest for general submission include, but are not limited to:
    • AI for Threat Detection & Response
    • Adversarial AI and Security
    • Privacy, Trust, and AI Security
    • AI in Authentication and Access Control
    • AI for Cloud & IoT Security
    • AI in Critical Infrastructure & National Security
    • AI in Cyber Warfare & Offensive Security
    • Quantum Computing, AI, and Cybersecurity
    • AI in Blockchain and Cryptographic Security
    • AI in Security Operations & SOC Automation
    • Large Language Models (LLMs) and Cybersecurity
    • Regulatory, Ethical, and Legal Aspects of AI in Cybersecurity
  • Important Dates: First Round
    • Full paper submission: 30-03-2025
    • Acceptance Notification : 06-04-2025
    • Registration (early-bird): 10-04-2025
    • Camera ready submission: 08-04-2025
