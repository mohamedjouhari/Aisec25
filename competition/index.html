What is Capture the Flag?
A Capture The Flag (CTF) competition is a cybersecurity challenge that tests participants' hacking skills.
The goal is to find hidden flags within a set time frame. Flags are usually hidden in vulnerable systems, applications, or networks.
Participants must use their hacking skills to exploit vulnerabilities and find the flags.
About AISEC CTF
This Event is a cybersecurity competition organized by CLUB GCDSTE at ENSA Marrakech as part of the AISEC event on Artificial Intelligence & Cybersecurity international summit
