About Our Event

The AiSec International Summit is an annual event organized by the Cyber Defense and Embedded Telecommunications Systems program of ENSA Marrakech. Its objective is to bring together cybersecurity stakeholders, researchers, students, professionals, and institutional decision-makers to discuss the latest advancements in cybersecurity and artificial intelligence. The event provides an opportunity for participants to share experiences, discover the latest technologies, and network with cybersecurity stakeholders.
